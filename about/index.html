You’ve stumbled upon netclectic.com, the personal site of one Adrian Cockburn.

Who is Adrian Cockburn?

Ask googlism and you get the followoing answer:

adrian cockburn is the mysterious “seventh arse” and his dual role is one of technological developer and drunken maniac heckler

In reality I was born in England in February 1972 to an English father and Scottish mother. Since my inception somewhere near a navy base in Chatham, Kent I have lived mostly in Scotland referring to a small town by the name of Helensburgh as my home town.

Currently living in Glasgow, I have been a software developer since leaving college in 1997. Although, in the summer of 2008 I moved jobs and broke away from the role of Software Developer into the role of Technical Architect at Vamosa. Vamosa was subsequently acquired by T-Systems in October 2010.

Incidentally, the googlism quote above refers to my mates band Tommy’s Magic Arse, whom I’ve tried to bring to a wider audience over the years via the wonders of the web.
